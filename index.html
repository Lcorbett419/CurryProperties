What we do

A simpler way to let your property

Curry Property Solutions Ltd is a rent-to-rent company. In plain terms, we become your tenant on a long-term contract and take full responsibility for the property. You receive a guaranteed rent every month, and we handle everything that usually comes with being a landlord.

It's letting without the lettings headache — no chasing rent, no void periods, no late-night maintenance calls. Just dependable income and a property kept in excellent condition.

The arrangement in short

  • ✓You stay the owner. Nothing about your ownership changes — we simply rent it from you.
  • ✓We pay a fixed rent. Agreed up front and paid on the same date each month.
  • ✓We manage everything. Tenants, upkeep, compliance and day-to-day issues are ours to handle.
  • ✓You're covered during voids. If the property sits empty, you're still paid in full.
